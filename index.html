Challenges

Problems with Existing Retail Systems

Most retailers still rely on disconnected tools for POS, inventory, logistics, and customer data—leading to inefficiencies, lost sales, and poor decision-making.

Disconnected Data Silos

Sales, inventory, and customer data live in separate systems that don't talk to each other.

Result: No single source of truth for the business.

Frequent Stock-Outs & Overstock

Without real-time demand visibility, popular products run out while slow-moving items pile up.

Result: Lost revenue and wasted inventory costs.

No Unified Customer View

Customer data is scattered across POS, e-commerce, and CRM tools.

Result: Poor personalization and weak customer retention.

Manual Reports & Excel Dependency

Teams rely on manual exports, spreadsheets, and delayed reports.

Result: Slow decisions and outdated insights.

Frequently Asked Questions

What retail systems does Gulabjamun connect with?
Gulabjamun integrates seamlessly with major POS systems, e-commerce platforms (Shopify, WooCommerce), and local warehouse management tools using our unified data pipeline.
How fast is the data processing?
Our system processes data in near real-time. Sales from your POS or e-commerce store typically reflect on your analytics dashboard within seconds.
Can I export my data to Excel?
Yes, Gulabjamun provides one-click exports for all reports. You can also edit and re-upload data via our smart spreadsheet integration.
Is my retail data secure?
Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption and offer role-based access control, ensuring only authorized personnel have visibility into sensitive financial data.
